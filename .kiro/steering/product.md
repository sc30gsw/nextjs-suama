# Product Overview

## Product Description

**nextjs-suama** is a modern work hours management and reporting system. It focuses on creating and managing daily and weekly reports, aiming to streamline business hour tracking and report creation processes.

### üìä Report Management

- **Daily Report Creation & Management**: Today's work content, time recording, progress tracking
- **Weekly Report Creation & Management**: Weekly business summaries, issues & solutions, time aggregation
- **Report Listing & Search**: Viewing past reports, filtering by date ranges

### üè¢ Report Context Management

- **Client Management**: Registration, editing, and deletion of client information
- **Project Management**: Registration, editing, and deletion of project information
- **Mission Management**: Registration, editing, and deletion of business missions
- **Appeal Category Management**: Management of achievement categories
- **Trouble Category Management**: Management of issue categories

### üë• User Management

- **User Registration & Editing**: Profile management, permission settings
- **Password Management**: Password change and reset functionality
- **Authentication System**: Secure authentication with Better Auth

### üìà Data Management & Analytics

- **Time Aggregation**: Automatic aggregation of daily and weekly work hours
- **CSV Import**: Bulk data registration functionality
- **Search & Filtering**: Advanced search and data filtering capabilities

## Target Use Case

### Primary Users

- **Individual Users**: Freelancers, consultants, remote workers
- **Small Teams**: Startups, small development teams
- **Project-Based Workers**: Professionals managing multiple concurrent projects

### Key Scenarios

1. **Daily Work Recording**: Accurate recording of daily work content and hours
2. **Weekly Report Creation**: Creating reports summarizing weekly achievements, issues, and solutions
3. **Client-Based Time Management**: Segregated time management for multiple clients
4. **Project Progress Tracking**: Visualization of project-specific progress and time allocation
